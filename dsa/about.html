About

A study guide, not a platform.

DSA Roadmap organizes 150 well-known LeetCode practice problems into 18 topics, arranged as a dependency roadmap: each topic unlocks the ones that build on it, so you always know what to study next instead of grinding a random list.

It is free and non-commercial. There are no ads, no accounts, no sign-ups and no tracking of any kind.

Your progress stays with you

Ticking a problem as done saves it to your browser's local storage — it never leaves your machine and is never sent anywhere. That also means progress is per-browser and per-device, and the “Reset progress” button in the header clears it for good.

Credit where it's due

The topic ordering and problem selection follow the excellent roadmap by NeetCode, and every problem lives on LeetCode. This site hosts no problem content — every problem simply links out to LeetCode, where you actually solve it. A few problems require LeetCode Premium; they are hidden by default and clearly badged when shown.

Found a dead link?

LeetCode occasionally renames or removes problems. Each problem row has a small “broken?” link that takes you to a LeetCode search for the title, so you can find where it moved.
